About FrontDesk Agents

We're on a mission to make AI-powered legal receptionist services accessible to law firms of every size.

Our Story

FrontDesk Agents was born from a simple frustration: too many law firms were losing potential clients after hours. Phone calls went to voicemail, intake forms were never completed, and there was no way to capture every lead.

We built FrontDesk Agents to solve that — an AI-powered receptionist designed specifically for law firms. 24/7 call handling. Smart case intake. Compliance-aware responses across all 50 states and 94 federal districts. All self-hosted so you maintain attorney-client privilege.

Today, FrontDesk Agents serves law firms ranging from solo practitioners to mid-size litigation groups, helping them capture every lead and deliver exceptional client experiences from the very first interaction.
