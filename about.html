About FrontDesk Agents

We're on a mission to make AI-powered legal receptionist services accessible to law firms of every size.

Our Story

FrontDesk Agents was born from a simple frustration: too many law firms were losing potential clients after hours. Phone calls went to voicemail, intake forms were never completed, and there was no way to capture every lead.

We built FrontDesk Agents to solve that — an AI-powered receptionist designed specifically for law firms. 24/7 call handling. Smart case intake. Compliance-aware responses across all 50 states. All self-hosted so you maintain attorney-client privilege.

What We Believe

🔒 Data Privacy First

Your customer data belongs to you, not us. Self-hosting means complete control over privacy and compliance.

⚡ Every Second Counts

Every missed call is a lost client. We build tools that help your team respond faster and capture every lead.

🤝 Human-First Automation

Automation should enhance human connection, not replace it. We automate the busywork so you can focus on relationships.

🌍 Accessible to All

Whether you're a solo practitioner or a large firm, our tools scale with you at a fair price.
