## 🎯 **HOW TO NAVIGATE**

### **From Homepage:**
1. Click **"Sign In"** (top right) → Customer Login
2. Click **"Order Now"** → Pricing section
3. Scroll to features → Features section
4. Scroll to AI Agents → Agent showcase
5. Scroll to Industries → Industry coverage
6. Scroll to Pricing → Pricing tiers
7. Scroll to testimonials → Customer stories
8. Click **"Owner Login"** (footer) → Owner Login

### **From Customer Login:**
1. Enter credentials → Access Customer Dashboard
2. Click **"Owner Login"** link → Owner Login page
3. Click **"Back to Home"** → Homepage

### **From Owner Login:**
1. Enter credentials → Access Owner Dashboard
2. Click **"Customer Login"** link → Customer Login
3. Click **"Back to Home"** → Homepage

### **From Owner Dashboard:**
1. View metrics (auto-refreshing)
2. Click **"Database"** → Supabase dashboard
3. Click **"Users"** → Supabase auth users
4. Click **"Refresh All"** → Reload data
5. Click **Theme Toggle** → Switch dark/light mode
6. Click **Owner Chat Button** → Open AI chat interface

### **From Customer Dashboard:**
1. View business metrics
2. Check call statistics
3. Monitor AI agents
4. View recent activity
